About Me

Hi, I’m Vanessa Burger! I’m an enthusiastic and reliable individual who enjoys balancing both physical and creative activities in my life. Whether it’s hitting the tennis courts, teaching riding lessons, or spending time in the great outdoors camping, I love staying active and embracing new challenges. I also enjoy visiting the gym and maintaining a healthy lifestyle.

In addition to my love for physical activities, I have a strong passion for creative expression. I find joy in sketching, painting, and crafting—anything that allows me to work with my hands and bring my ideas to life.

I pride myself on being friendly, approachable, and dependable. I’m also very organized and manage my time well, always eager to learn and grow in every aspect of my personal and professional life. Above all, I am deeply family-oriented and value spending quality time with my loved ones, creating memories and supporting each other along the way.
